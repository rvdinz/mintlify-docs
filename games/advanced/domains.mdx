## Custom Domain Setup

Learn how to connect a custom domain to your server. (e.g. mc.xahosting.com)

<Tip>
  We would recommend [**Cloudflare**](https://cloudflare.com/) for this setup, as changes apply within minutes!
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="A Record">
    - **Type:** A
    - **Name:** `@` (optional; add a prefix like `mc` for mc.xahosting.com)
    - **IPv4 Address:** Server IP (Numerical)
    - **Proxy Status:** DNS Only
    - **TTL:** Auto

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius:"0.5rem" }}
      src="/public/images/image-13.png"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="SRV Record">
    - **Type:** SRV
    - **Name:** `_minecraft._tcp`
    - **Priority:** 0
    - **Weight:** 0
    - **TTL:** Auto
    - **Port:** Your Server Port
    - **Target** Full host `(e.g. mc.xahosting.com or xahosting.com)`

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius:"0.5rem" }}
      src="/public/images/image-14.png"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    You may need to wait anywhere from 5 minutes up to 24 hours for the changes to take effect. We recommend checking every hour. If you need further assistance, please open a ticket on our [**Discord**](https://discord.xahosting.co/) or through the [**Billing Portal**](https://billing.xahosting.co/tickets)."
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Subdomain Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Game Panel">
    - Open [**panel.xahosting.co**](https://panel.xahosting.co) in your browser.
    - Select your server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigation">
    Scroll down and click **More**, then select **Subdomain**. After that, go to the top-right corner and press `Create Subdomain`.

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius:"0.5rem" }}
      src="/public/images/image-15.png"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Subdomain">
    Enter your desired subdomain prefix (e.g., `llama.xahost.co`), select a domain from our available options, choose the allocation, and click **Create**.

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius:"0.5rem" }}
      src="/public/images/image-16.png"
    />

    Your subdomain will be ready to use!

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius:"0.5rem" }}
      src="/public/images/image-17.png"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  You have a domain/subdomain setup on your server and can use that to join!
</Check>
