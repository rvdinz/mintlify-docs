## Quick Launch
Easily connect to your server with a single click from the panel.
<Steps>
  <Step title="Download an SFTP Client">
    Choose and install one of the following SFTP clients:

    - **[WinSCP](https://winscp.net/eng/index.php)**
    - **[FileZilla](https://filezilla-project.org/)**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch SFTP from the Panel">
    - Go to our **[game panel](https://panel.xahosting.co/)**, log in, and click the server you want to SFTP in.
    - Navigate to **Settings**.
    - Click the **Launch STFP** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>If this doesn’t work, you can find a more in-depth tutorial below.</Warning>

## Manual Setup
Step-by-step instructions for connecting using your SFTP client and credentials.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download an SFTP Client">
    Choose and install one of the following SFTP clients:

    - **[WinSCP](https://winscp.net/eng/index.php)**
    - **[FileZilla](https://filezilla-project.org/)**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retrieve Your SFTP Credentials">
    Log in to the XA Hosting server panel. Navigate to your server’s **Settings** section and locate your SFTP details. These include:

    - **Host** (e.g., fn1.gs.xahosting.co)
    - **Username** (e.g., johndoe.15kf9le6)
    - **Port** (e.g., 2022)
    - **Password** (your panel account password)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Using Your SFTP Client">
    **For FileZilla:**
    1. Open FileZilla.
    2. Enter your credentials in the top toolbar:
       - Host: Your server’s SFTP address (e.g., fn1.gs.xahosting.co)
       - Username: Your full username (including the short server ID)
       - Password: Your panel password
       - Port: The provided port (e.g., 2022)
    3. Click **Quickconnect** to establish the connection.

    **For WinSCP:**
    1. Open WinSCP and select **New Session**.
    2. Fill in the fields:
       - File Protocol: SFTP
       - Host name: Your server’s SFTP address
       - Port number: The provided port
       - User name: Your full username
       - Password: Your panel password
    3. Click **Login** to connect.
  </Step>
</Steps>
