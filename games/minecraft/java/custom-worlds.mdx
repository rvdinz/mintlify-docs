description: 'Upload or install custom Minecraft worlds on your XA Hosting server.'

<Warning>If you run into any issues while setting up a world, the XA Hosting support team is here to help.</Warning>

<Steps>
    <Step title="Get Your World Ready">
    - Make sure your custom world folder is complete and compatible with your server version.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Upload the World">
    - Connect to your server using an **[SFTP client](/games/sftp)**.  
    - Upload your world folder directly into the serverâ€™s root directory.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Match the Version">
    - In your **Dashboard**, open the **Version Changer**.  
    - Select the same version that your custom world was built on.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Restart and Play">
    - Restart the server to load your custom world.  
    - Enjoy your new environment with friends!
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>You successfully installed your custom world!</Check>
