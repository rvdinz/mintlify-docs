--- 
title: 'Server Icon'
description: 'Learn how to customize your server icon with just one click!'
icon: 'print'
---

<Steps>
    <Step title="Navigate to Files">
    In the top-right corner, you will see several buttons. Click on the one labeled `Change Icon`.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Pick Your Icon/Logo">
    After clicking the button, choose the icon or logo you want to use for your server.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Restart Your Server">
    Restart your server to apply the new server icon.
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>Players will now see your customized server icon when joining the server.</Check>
