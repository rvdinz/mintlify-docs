---
title: 'Two-Factor Authentication'
description: 'Protect your account by enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for an extra layer of security.'
icon: 'shield'
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the Billing Portal">
    Visit **[billing.xahosting.co](https://billing.xahosting.co/)** in your browser. Make sure you are **[logged in](/portal/get-started)**.

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/public/images/image-1.png"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Security Settings">
    - Click your profile picture at the top of the page.  
    - Select `Account` from the dropdown menu.  

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/public/images/image-9.png"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable Two-Factor Authentication">
    - Locate the `Security` section in the left sidebar.  

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/public/images/image-10.png"
    />

    - Click **Enable 2FA**.

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/public/images/image-11.png"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Scan QR Code with Your Authenticator App">
    - Open an authenticator app (e.g., Google Authenticator, Authy, Microsoft Authenticator).  
    - Use the app to scan the QR code shown on the page.  
    - Enter the 6-digit verification code from the app to confirm setup.  
  </Step>   
</Steps>

<Check>
  Two-Factor Authentication is now enabled! Your account is protected with an extra layer of security.
</Check>
