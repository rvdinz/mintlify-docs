---
title: 'Cancel'
description: 'Cancel your active plans anytime in just a few clicks.'
icon: 'ban'
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Billing Portal">
    Visit **[billing.xahosting.co](https://billing.xahosting.co/)** in your browser. Make sure to be **[logged in](/portal/get-started)**.

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/public/images/image-1.png"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Your Dashboard">
    - Click your profile picture at the top of the page.  
    - Then, click `Dashboard`.  

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/public/images/image-2.png"
    />

    - You will be redirected to a page showing your `Active Services`, `Unpaid Invoices`, and `Support Tickets`.  

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/public/images/image-3.png"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Your Plan">
    - Locate the service you want to cancel.  

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/public/images/image-4.png"
    />

    - Click on your plan, then click the `Cancel` button.  

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/public/images/image-7.png"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Cancellation">
    - A pop up will appear, that shows the `Cancellation Type` and the `Reason of Cancellation`.
    - Submit the form and you are done!  

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/public/images/image-8.png"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  You have successfully cancelled you plan in a few steps.
</Check>
